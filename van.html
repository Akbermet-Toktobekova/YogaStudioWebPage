A dynamic and energetic yoga class that links breath with movement.

About Vinyasa Flow

Vinyasa Flow is a style of yoga characterized by stringing poses together seamlessly so that you move from one to another, using breath. Often referred to as "flow" yoga, this style offers a dynamic, creative sequence of movements that challenge the body and calm the mind.

Key Benefits:

  • Improves flexibility and strength.
  • Boosts cardiovascular health.
  • Encourages mindfulness and reduces stress.
  • Helps to detoxify the body through breath and movement.

What to Expect in a Vinyasa Flow Class:

Our Vinyasa Flow classes are suitable for all levels, from beginners to experienced practitioners. Each class begins with a gentle warm-up, moves into a dynamic flow sequence, and concludes with a calming cool-down and relaxation. Modifications are offered for poses to suit individual needs and skill levels.
